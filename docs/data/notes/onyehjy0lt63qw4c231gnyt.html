Group Ownership

Group ownership and integrated ownership model

  • (Model). Group ownership.

  • Definition (Business group). Define entities in a business group as a group of firms which have cross-shareholdings with one another, i.e. they own each other: e.g. AAA owns 10% of BBB, B4owns10B4 owns 10% of $CB4owns10, CCC owns 20% of AAA.

    • Definition. (External shareholder) There will also be an external shareholder: a firm which has direct ownership over all the firms in the group, but is not owned by the group.

    • Definition (Intrinsic value). Let v\mathbf{v}v be the column vector denoting the intrinsic value (market cap, revenue, or some other metric) of each firm in the group

    • Let d\mathbf{d}d be the row vector denoting the percentage of shares the external shareholder owns for each firm in the group, with portfolio value pext=dvp_{ext}=\mathbf{dv}pext​=dv

    • Definition (Group value). The group value vector is defined recursively as vG=WGvG+v\mathbf{v^G} =\mathbf{W^G v^G +v}vG=WGvG+v

      • Interpretation: the group value of a firm is its intrinsic value (v\mathbf{v}v) plus the weighted sum of its ownership in other firms (WGvG\mathbf{W^G v^G}WGvG)

      • (Theorem.) Reorganizing gives the solution vG=(I−WG)−1vv^G=(I-W^G)^{-1}vvG=(I−WG)−1v. (1)

      • We also have the group value of the external shareholder, vextG=vext+dvGv^G_{ext}=v_{ext}+dv^GvextG​=vext​+dvG where vextv_{ext}vext​ is the intrinsic value of the shareholder

      • We write vtotGv^G_{tot}vtotG​ to be the vector that comprises the group value of the shareholder and the group's firms: vtotG=(vextGvG)v^G_{tot}=\begin{pmatrix}v^G_{ext} \\ v^G\end{pmatrix}vtotG​=(vextG​vG​)

      • Definition (Integrated ownership). Integrated ownership refers to the sum of the direct and indirect ownership of a shareholder in a firm.

      • For the external shareholder in this group, let the integrated ownership be d^\hat{d}d^. Then we have d^=d+d^WG\hat{d}=d+\hat{d}W^Gd^=d+d^WG, defined similarly as the group value vector

        • Interpretation: ddd is the external ownership, and d^WG\hat{d} W^Gd^WG is the internal ownership (the matrix WGW^GWG representing the ownership of firms within the group, weighted by the internal ownership of the shareholder over the firms d^\hat{d}d^)
        • (Theorem.) Thus we also have d^=d(I−WG)−1\hat{d}=d(I-W^G)^{-1}d^=d(I−WG)−1. (2)

      • (Theorem.) d^v=dvG\hat{d}v = dv^Gd^v=dvG.

        • (Proof). d^v=d(1−WG)−1v=dvG\hat{d}v=d(1-W^G)^{-1}v=dv^Gd^v=d(1−WG)−1v=dvG by (1) and (2).
        • Interpretation: Direct ownership portfolio of shareholder over group values = integrated ownership portfolio over underlying values
