Prediction in Complex Systems

Vidmer et al., 2015

A diffusion algorithm is used to predict the evolution of the multiproduct WTN, using a new measure of WTN fitness and product similarity.

Literature review

  • Complex network properties of the WTN first exhibited in Topology of the World Trade Web
  • Gravity model fitted to WTN in Gravity and Rich-Club Phenomenon
  • Product Space in The Product Space Conditions the Development of Nations + Economic Complexity in Hausmann et al. 2006
  • Export predictions done in Hummels and Klenow 2005
  • A recommender system can be used to predict future exports and exported products (The Product Space Conditions the Development of Nations, Tacchella et al. 2013, "A new measure for countries' fitness and product complexity")
    • Originally used in online systems/social media networks (Lv and Zhou 2011, "Link prediction in complex networks: a survey")
    • Use heat diffusion/mass diffusion model + economic complexity
